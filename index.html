Spreadsheet Transformer

Instructions

  1. Click Upload to choose an Excel or CSV file.
  2. After uploading, preview the data.
  3. Click My Fixed Data Range / Columns to specify columns that should remain unchanged.
  4. Click Triplet Data Range to specify columns for Brand, Subcategory, and SKU data.
  5. Click Process to normalize the data.
  6. Click Download Excel to export the result.
